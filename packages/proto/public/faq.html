Questions with Helpful Responses

  • Do you have to create an account to make a post?
    • Yes, you must make an account to post, as it is a security and verification process.
  • Do you have to log in to see reviews?
    • No, it is public, so you don't need to make an account or log in.
  • What does the rating mean?
    • The rating is an average of all the ratings for that specific major.
  • What does it mean by program in the post page?
    • In the case of the post page, the program mean whether you are in the undergraduate(1-4 year), Master(after undergrad at somewhere else), and Blended (5-6 year at Cal Poly).
  • How can I believe the reviews?
    • For the most part, most reviewers will be verified Cal Poly Students before making a post, but there are loop holes, hence there may be occassional cases of falsify reviews, but will try to mitigate such issues.
  • How does verification work?
    • The user will be asked for a calpoly email and will be sent an email that they physically need to agree to.
  • What is a review?
    • It is a summary and opinionated response to your Cal Poly specific major.
  • Should I believe the review?
    • Every review should be taken with a grain of salt such as it could be true, but can also be untrue, not necessarily false, as the reviews are based upon opinions.
  • Can posts be removed?
    • At the current stage, posts will not be remove, so please make sure if you are a reviewer and making a post, make sure you oblige by the rules.
  • Have other questions?
    • Submit a question here. Will get back to you in a 3 business days.
